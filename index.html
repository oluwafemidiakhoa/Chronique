About Chroniq

Truth, memory, and stewardship in one archive

Chroniq exists to preserve truth in people's own words, with integrity, security, and consent.

Mission

Record meaningful interviews with care

Capture stories, reflections, profiles, and testimony for release now or for a future moment chosen in advance.

Vision

Build the most trusted interview archive network

Create a category defined by respect, secure custody, and clarity around consent across many interview formats.

Belief
