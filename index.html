About Chroniq

Chroniq exists to preserve truth in people’s own words — with integrity, security, and consent.

Mission

Preserve and release legacy-defining interviews both present and posthumously.

Vision

Become the most trusted legacy archive network.

What we believe

People don’t just leave behind assets — they leave behind meaning. Chroniq records life stories, values, lessons, and truth with care, then protects them with a clear consent framework and secure storage. Some stories are meant to be shared now. Others should be sealed until the right moment.

How it works

Two models. One standard: integrity.

Chroniq Vault

Recorded, sealed, and released posthumously

Your interview is recorded, securely sealed, and legally protected. It’s released only after demise according to your written consent and instructions.

  • Consent & release instructions
  • Secure storage & controlled access
  • Release triggered only by agreed conditions
Chroniq Live

Recorded and released immediately

Your interview is recorded and edited for public release — ideal for professionals, creators, founders, leaders, and community voices building impact today.

  • Professional recording & editing
  • Distribution-ready clips
  • Respectful, legacy-centered storytelling
